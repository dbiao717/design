GI is an interactive production studio belonging to Gwantsi Productions. We deliver motion and interactive content from concept to completion. 

We have been pushing the boundaries of what is possible since the studio was set up. Our employees' passion is only exceeded by their talent. We aim to make Gwantsi Interactive a great place to work and a great partner to work with. 

Our team and skills range from software developers to CG artists who have deep insight for applying complex technology solutions to the creative process.
